Chicken

Chicken is a major worldwide source of meat and eggs for human consumption. It was one of the basic ingredients in the so-called white dish, a stew usually consisting of chicken and fried onions cooked in milk and seasoned with spices and sugar.

Pork

Pork is the culinary name for the meat of a domestic pig (Sus scrofa domesticus). It is the most commonly consumed meat worldwide, with evidence of pig husbandry dating back to 5000 BC. Pork is eaten both freshly cooked and preserved to get different flavor.

Sushi

Nigiri consists of thinly sliced, raw fish pressed on top of sushi rice. There are some forms of nigiri that have cooked or seared fish, if you can't bring yourself to eat it raw. Common types of nigiri include sake (salmon), ahi (tuna), and ebi (shrimp), but there are several kinds.
